Frequently Asked Questions

+ I don’t own a smartphone. Do I have to buy one to use Coracare’s services?


For now, yes you have to get a smartphone. If you send us an e-mail with your queries we will be able to help you choose one that is suitable for you. In the near future, however, we also plan to expand our services to allow users to use a watch instead of a smartphone.


+ What is the cost of the Coracare service package per annum?


Each user pays an annual fee of £250.


+ I have a busy lifestyle and cannot afford wasting a lot of time fixing and regulating the device. Is Coracare suitable for me?


Coracare is suitable for anyone with a heart condition. The silicon slab is small and lightweight, very easy to put on. It is not cumbersome at all so you can go on with your daily activities as normal. The only maintenance involved is changing the battery once a month which can easily be done during a TV advert.


+ How often do I have to change the battery?


The battery lasts about a month if you use non-rechargeable ones and lasts around two weeks if you have decided to use our rechargeable batteries.

+ Which batteries am I using?


The batteries in the CX50 are coin cell batteries and the name is etched on them. They are either the rechargeable GP320BVH or the non-rechargeable CR2477.


+ Do your services work outside the UK?


Unfortunately, for the time being we only provide services in the UK. However, we hope to expand our services worldwide in the future.

+ I cannot watch the video on the website. What can I do?


Please install Adobe Flash to view them.


+ When is the expected release date?


We aim to release our product in the UK market within the next two years.


+ Can you customize the Coracare service?


Yes, your doctor will be able to customize the algorithms that analyse your ECG scans to best suit your condition.


+ How can I access my ECG scans history?


Coracare provides its users with a simple and efficient way to track their ECG scans history. Your doctor will also have access to these records. Please login to view them.


+ Who uses Coracare services?


Those at risk of a heart attack may be advised by their doctors to wear the CX50 ECG patch.


+ What is the service Coracare offers?


Coracare provides the CX50 ECG patch that monitors the user; the data collection, secure transmission and storage in a Coracare server; and the data-user interface provided on this website.


+ Why use Coracare?


The Coracare service increases the chance of survival of a heart attack and reduces the time spent in hospital which is beneficial to the user, user’s loved ones, health care provider and (indirectly) the economy.


+ I have forgotten my password what can I do?


You will need to email Sebastian Grubb - sebastian.grubb10@imperial.ac.uk with your Coracare user name and email.


+ I have trouble using the CX50 who can I contact?


First please check that your battery has been replaced in the past month if you are using no-rechargeable batteries or in the past 2 weeks if you are using rechargeable batteries, if it has not please replace it. If you still have problems please call our helpline and they will be happy to help you.
