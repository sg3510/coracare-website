About Coracare

We're a group of 8 students at Imperial College London researching the feasibility of a remote heart monitoring system and how it could improve the lives of people on a daily basis. Past heart monitors have been considered uncomfortable and can only be used for monitoring purposes. Coracare aims to offer a full service comprising of a comfortable heart monitor (the CX50) and state of the art monitoring and diagnosis for heart problems.
